Who are we?

We are the wandering circus family, the lonely but yet not so lonely family.

We are the sinners, the trapped and the damned souls with no destination and we will only regain our freedom if we get replaced by innocent souls.

For hundreds of years we have walked this cursed road and entertained simpleminded people with our clowns and animals and we are destined to continue this path until our souls are released.
